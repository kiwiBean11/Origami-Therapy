How to Make a Lotus Flower!

The lotus flower symbolizes purity, spiritual awakening, and rebirth. In religions like Buddhism and Hinduism, the lotus flower is connected to awakening and transcending as the bud rises from the muddy water into the clean fresh air to bloom. White, pink, or blue lotuses symbolize purity, compassion, or spiritual power. In Ancient Egypt the lotus was believed to be connected to Ra, the god of the sun since it opens at dawn and closes at night, similar to the sunrise and sunset.
